REST DAY

Recovery is where the growth happens. Your muscles repair and grow stronger while you rest. Honor this day as much as your training days.

💧
Hydration
Aim for 3–4 litres of water today. Proper hydration speeds up muscle recovery and flushes metabolic waste.
🥩
Protein Intake
Hit your protein goals even on rest days. Aim for 0.8–1g per pound of bodyweight to support muscle protein synthesis.
🧘
Stretching
Spend 15–20 minutes on light stretching or yoga. Focus on tight areas from this week's training.
😴
Sleep Quality
Aim for 7–9 hours. Growth hormone is released during deep sleep — this is literally when you get bigger.
